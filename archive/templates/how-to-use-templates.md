# How to use templates

1. Copy `incident-template.md` or `postmortem-template.md` into a new investigation folder under `investigations/`.
2. Copy `runbook-template.md` into `operations/` or `demo/` as needed.
3. Copy `adr-template.md` into `adrs/`.
4. Use `chaos-test-template.md` before marking chaos experiments Complete.
